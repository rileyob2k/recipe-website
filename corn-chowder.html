Corn Chowder

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter (2 sticks)
  • 1 small carrot, finely diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3 cups white corn, fresh or frozen
  • 3 cups stock, chicken or vegetable
  • 2 cups half-and-half
  • 3/4th to 1 ½ cups milk
  • ¾ to 1 ½ cups cheese, shredded (to taste)
  • Pinch nutmeg

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Melt 1 stick butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, carrot, celery and garlic. Saute for 2 minutes.
  2. Add flour to above step, stir to make roux cook until lightly brown. Set aside to cool.
  3. Combine corn and stock in another saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Whisk corn/stock into roux and bring to a boil. Mixture should become thick.
  5. Pour half-and-half into now empty saucepan, gently heat to steaming. Stir half and half into the corn pixture.
  6. Bring mixture to boil. Add nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Add cheese and milk to desired consistency and taste.
  8. Just before serving, cut remaining butter into chunks. Add to soup, stirring to melt butter.
