One Hour Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup + 2 tablespoon hot water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup Crisco
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 1/4c sugar
  • 1 egg

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Mix water, salt, sugar, crisco, then egg.
  2. After mixing in egg, add 1 tbsp yeast and 3 ½ cups flour.
  3. Knead all dough with dough hook until smooth for about 5 minutes. It should start to pull away from the edges and start to form a ball. It should be tacky, but not sticky. If sticky, add ¼ cup flour.
  4. Seperate dough into 5 section, then each section into 3 pieces. Roll each into ball, pinch bottoms.
  5. Put into greased 13x9 pan and cover with plastic wrap sprayed with Pam.
  6. Let rise for one hour.
  7. Remove plastic and bake @425 for 12 minutes. It should be evenly brown while baking.
  8. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter and brush on rolls when out of oven.
