Corn Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 3 T. cornstarch
  • ½ to ¾ c. sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 (14.75oz) can cream style corn
  • 1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
  • ¼ tsp. Salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the cornstarch and sugar with a whisk. Add eggs and beat well.
  2. Add corn and evaporated milk; mix well. Add salt.
  3. Pour into a greased 1 ½ qt. Casserole dish.
  4. Dot the top with butter slices.
  5. Bake @ 350 for approximately 60 mins. Until golden brown and set.
