Special Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. Medium cut elbow (or shaped) macaroni
  • ½ lb sharp cheddar block, shredded
  • ½ lb Mont-Jack cheese block, shredded
  • 1 lb. Block original Velveeta, cubed
  • 1 ¼ cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • salt/pepper to taste
  • Cajun or mixed up seasoning to taste
  • 1 stick butter, sliced.

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Spray 4 qt. Baking dish
  2. Boil noodles, rinse, drain, set aside.
  3. Pre-heat oven to 375
  4. Shred cheeses, set aside
  5. Pour milk into medium sauce pan. Warm over medium heat. Add velvetta, stir until cheese is melted. Add additional milk if necessary to thin sauce.
  6. In a small seperate bowl, crack the eggs, lightly whisk. Add milk cheese mix to egg, stirring to temper eggs, continually whisking. Once combined, add egg to cheese sauce.
  7. Place ½ of cooked pasta to pan. Season lightly, with salt, pepper and cajun seasoning. Slice thin pieces of butter, add on top of pasta. Sprinkle ½ of the shredded cheese on top of noodles. Ladle ½ of the cheese sauce over layer. Repeat for second layer.
  8. Cook @ 375 for about 30-35 mins
